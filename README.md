## 📄 About This Review

This repository contains the full review paper on green and sustainable energy technologies, 
covering developments from 2020 to 2025. The paper synthesizes peer-reviewed literature, 
intergovernmental reports (IEA, IRENA, IPCC), and policy documents.

## 🔑 Key Findings

- **Solar PV LCOE** fell by **87%** (2010–2022), reaching ~$0.049/kWh
- **Onshore wind LCOE** fell by **68%**, reaching ~$0.033/kWh  
- **Perovskite solar cells** achieved certified efficiencies >**26%**
- **Li-ion battery costs** declined to ~**$115/kWh** (2024)
- **Global clean energy investment** exceeded **$2 trillion** in 2024
